Methodology notes

  • The 17 QoL scores are composite indices computed by Teleport from multiple sub-indicators. Exact weighting is proprietary.
  • Numbeo data is user-reported; accuracy may vary in less-represented cities.
  • Monetary values are in USD and not inflation-adjusted. Since all figures are from roughly the same period, the effect on comparisons is negligible.
  • The dataset is anchored on Teleport's city list (289 cities). Cities missing any attribute after merging were dropped, introducing a bias toward larger, well-documented cities.
  • Data reflects a static snapshot and is not updated in real time.
